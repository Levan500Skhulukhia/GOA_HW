Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance

  • Release date: February 19, 2013
  • Platforms: PC, PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, and later re-released for other platforms
  • Gente: Action, Hack-and-Slash
  • Developer: PlatinumGames
  • Publisher: Konami

Overview

Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance is a high-octane action game that blends intense hack-and-slash combat with stealth elements. It serves as a spin-off to the iconic Metal Gear series, shifting focus from stealth gameplay to fast-paced, adrenaline-filled combat. In this installment, players take control of Raiden, a cyborg ninja armed with a high-frequency blade capable of slicing through anything in his path

Story

Set four years after the events of Metal Gear Solid 4: Guns of the Patriots, the game follows Raiden as he battles a private military company known as Desperado Enforcement LLC. Raiden must uncover a sinister plot involving cyborg enhancements and human trafficking while embracing his own complex identity as a soldier and a protector. The story explores themes of morality, identity, and the consequences of war in a futuristic world.
