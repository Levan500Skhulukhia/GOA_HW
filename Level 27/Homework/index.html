Top 3 movies of 2024

  1. "Dune: Part Two"

    2. Directed by Denis Villeneuve, this epic sequel continues the story of Paul Atreides (played by Timothée Chalamet ) as he rises among the Fremen and confronts his destiny. The film is packed with breathtaking visuals, large-scale battles, and stunning sandworm sequences. It's a must-watch for fans of sci-fi and world-building

  2. "Perfect Days"

    3. Directed by Wim Wenders, this quiet, reflective film follows a Tokyo toilet cleaner named Hirayama. Through his routine, moments of introspection, and interactions, the movie offers a deeply touching exploration of life’s simplicity and beauty

  3. "Love Lies Bleeding

    4. A queer crime thriller by Rose Glass, starring Kristen Stewart, this film blends supernatural elements with raw emotion. It tells the story of gym manager Lou and her entangled relationship with a bodybuilder, set against the backdrop of 1980s Albuquerque. The movie stands out for its intense chemistry and gripping storytelling​
